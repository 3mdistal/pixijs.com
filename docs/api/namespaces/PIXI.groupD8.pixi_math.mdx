[@pixi/math](../modules/pixi_math).[PIXI](../namespaces/PIXI).groupD8


Implements the dihedral group D8, which is similar to [group D4](http&#58;//mathworld.wolfram.com/DihedralGroupD4.html); D8 is the same but with diagonals, and it is used for texture rotations.

The directions the U- and V- axes after rotation of an angle of `a: GD8Constant` are the vectors `(uX(a), uY(a))`and `(vX(a), vY(a))`. These aren't necessarily unit vectors.

**Origin&#58;**This is the small part of gameofbombs.com portal system. It works.
